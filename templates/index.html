About us

MakeUrChoice is an initiative by 4 students of IIIT Kottayam to change the way of regular voting. This is an idea by M Sai Vamsi which brings out a new vision of choosing a particular option by a large number of people. The team consists of people who believe the current system to be flawed and want to change the system. The idea was executed by the team consisting of M Sai Vamsi, Prajjwal Jogdand, Sarthak Bindal and Harsh Raj.
